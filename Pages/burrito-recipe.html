Burrito Recipe

Some information about the dish

    Ingredients

  1. One Onion

  2. 400g of tin chopped tomatoes

  3. Two tablespoon tomato pur√©e

  4. One tablespoon of sunflower oil

  5. Two Gloves of Garlic

  6. One & a half teaspoon of paprika

  7. One & a half teaspoon of cumin

  8. 500g quality minced beef

  9. 250mls organic beef stock

  10. 10 tortillas

  11. 75ml beef stock

  12. 100g of cheddar cheese

    Instructions

  1. Add oil to a pan and bring to the heat. Add the chopped onions to the pan to fry until golden brown. Then add the garlic and spices and cook for 3 minutes. Then add the mince and cook until browned.

  2. Then peel the garlic cloves and finely dice them, then add it some paprika and cumin for flavour, and cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Once that is done then added the beef to the pan until it is browned.

  3. Once the meat has been browned then add in the stock and chopped tomatoes. Bring to the boil and then reduce the heat so it simmers and cook until it has thickened.

  4. Heat the grill and place the filling into the tortillas centres. Cover them in cheese and fold the sides of the tortillas to cover the middle.

  5. Once the cheese has melted, enjoy the burritos!
