Italian Recipies

Risotto
Risotto is an Italian dish that comes from northon Italy that is made from short grain rice with a beef or vegtable stock, butter, onions and white wine.

Pizza
Margherita pizza is a tradintional pizza dish from Naples Italy which is a dough base topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese and basil.

Rating:
Lasagne
Lasagne is an Italian dish that is made from sheets of pasta stacked on top of each other with a filling in the middle. This filling is made from meats and cheese.

Rating:

Polenta
Polenta is an Italian dish that is made from grinding corn into a flour. Which is then cooked to a creamy and thick texture. It can be ate like a porridge or left to set into a bread/pasta form.

Rating:
Ravioli
Ravioli are an Italian dish which are a square or round pillow shaped pasta that are filled with a stuffing. These fillings consist of meats, cheese and vegtables, the meat can be removed for a vegetarian option.

Rating:
Focaccia
Focaccia is an Italian flat bread dish that is either sweet or savory. Focaccia is like pizza crust that is 1 inch thick and flavoured in oil and covered in vegetables. The bread will have small indentations made to store the flavour.

Rating:

Carbonara
Carbonara is a pasta dish that contains eggs, hard cheese, meat and black pepper. The cheese that is usually used is Pecorino Romano, Parmigiano-Reggiano, or a mixture.

Rating:
Tiramisu
Tiramisu is an Italian dessert which is made from ladyfinger cookies that are soaked in coffee and liquor, cheese, and topped with cocoa powder.

Rating:
Ossobuco
Ossobuco is an Italian dish that is made from veal shanks that are braised in wine and seasonal stock cubes. Flavoured by cinnamon & gremolata. Served alongside vegtables.
