Italian Recipies

Risotto
Risotto is an Italian dish that comes from northon Italy that is made from short grain rice with a beef or vegtable stock, butter, onions and white wine.

Pizza
Margherita pizza is a tradintional pizza dish from Naples Italy which is a dough base topped with tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese and basil.

Rating:
Lasagne
Lasagne is an Italian dish that is made from sheets of pasta stacked on top of each other with a filling in the middle. This filling is made from meats and cheese.

Rating:

Polenta
Polenta is an Italian dish that iss made from grinding corn into a flour. Which is then cooked to a creamy and thick texture. It can be ate like a porridge or left to set into a bread/pasta form.

Rating:
Ravioli
Ravioli are an Italian dish which are a square or round pillow shaped pasta that are filled with a stuffing. These fillings consist of meats, cheese and vegtables, the meat can be removed for a vegetarian option.

Rating:
Focaccia
Focaccia is an Italian flat bread similar to pizza dough that can be either sweet or savory. Focaccia is usually 1/2 to 1 inch thick, with a light crust and an surface full of indentations that hold oil for extra flavour.

Rating:

Carbonara
Carbonara is a pasta dish made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork and black pepper. The cheese that is usually used is Pecorino Romano, Parmigiano-Reggiano, or a mixture.

Rating:
Tiramisu
Tiramisu is an Italian dessert which is made from delicate ladyfinger cookies, espresso or instant espresso, mascarpone cheese, eggs, sugar, Marsala wine, rum and cocoa powder.

Rating:
Ossobuco
Ossobuco is an Italian dish that is made from veal shanks that are braised with vegtables, white wine and seasonal stock cubes. Flavoured by cinnamon & gremolata.
